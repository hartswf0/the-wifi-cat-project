About the Film

Through a blend of AI-driven film production and classic narrative techniques, "Save the Wi-Fi Cat" explores the intersection of human relationships, digital disconnection, and the power of storytelling. The film takes inspiration from cybernetic cineosis, merging the digital with the emotional.
